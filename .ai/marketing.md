# QuickTools.ai - Marketing Strategy

## 1. Organic SEO (The Secret Weapon)
- **Auto-Blogging:** Use the Vercel cron job to post daily AI-generated articles.
- **Target Keywords:** Focus on long-tail keywords like "best free ai image generator for marketers" or "remove background without losing quality".
- **Internal Linking:** Every blog post MUST link back to the 5 core AI tools.

## 2. Product Launch Platforms
- **ProductHunt:** Launch V1 with a clear "Free Tier" offering to get upvotes.
- **AI Directories:** Submit to Toolify.ai, There's an AI for That (TAAFT), and Futurepedia.
- **HackerNews:** Share the technical story ("How a solo dev built 5 AI tools with Cursor & Next.js in 1 week").

## 3. Social Media & Communities
- **Reddit:** Post in r/SideProject, r/SaaS, and r/OpenAI without being too promotional. Ask for feedback.
- **Twitter/X:** Build in public. Share traffic stats and updates to gain a developer/founder following.
- **Pinterest:** Pin the generated HD images (from AI Image Generator) with links back to the site.
